Fundamentals of Basketball

What is Basketball?

Basketball is a high-energy sport where two teams of five players compete to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent’s hoop. The game combines athleticism, strategy, and teamwork, making it one of the most popular sports in the world.

Each game is played on a rectangular court with a hoop on each end. Players must dribble, pass, and shoot the ball while also defending against the opposing team. It’s a great way to build coordination, endurance, and teamwork skills.

Essential Basketball Skills

To play basketball effectively, you must master several key skills that improve your control, accuracy, and confidence on the court.

  • Dribbling
  • Shooting
  • Passing
  • Defense
  • Rebounding

Steps to Improve Your Dribbling

  1. Start with basic stationary dribbles.
  2. Switch hands and dribble while walking.
  3. Keep your eyes up, not on the ball.
  4. Increase speed and move around defenders.
